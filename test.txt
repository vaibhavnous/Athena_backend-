 Rewritten BRD (Optimized for Your System)

1. Executive Summary
The Insurance Operations & Financial Intelligence Platform aims to provide a unified data foundation for analyzing core insurance processes including policy management, claims processing, and financial transactions.
The Bronze Layer captures raw operational data from upstream systems without transformation, preserving full transactional detail for downstream analytics.
The platform integrates data across:


Policy lifecycle events


Premium collections


Claims registration and processing


Claim payments and reserve estimations


This layer serves as the authoritative source of granular insurance data, enabling accurate computation of operational and financial metrics.
Scope:
Management of Delta tables within workspace.bronze schema under Unity Catalog, sourced from existing insurance systems.

2. Business Context
Insurance operations involve multiple interconnected processes such as policy issuance, claim handling, and financial settlements. These processes generate high-volume transactional data across different systems.
To enable effective monitoring and decision-making, the organization requires:


Visibility into claims processing timelines


Tracking of premium inflows vs claim outflows


Monitoring of policy lifecycle activities


Measurement of financial exposure and reserves


The Bronze layer enables this by consolidating raw data across all operational systems.

3. Business Objectives

3.1 Claims Processing Monitoring


Track claim lifecycle from registration to settlement

Identify delays and bottlenecks



3.2 Financial Flow Tracking


Monitor premium collections across policies


Track claim payments and settlement amounts


Compare reserves with actual payouts



3.3 Policy Activity Analysis


Analyze policy issuance, updates, and transactions


Track policy-level financial contributions



3.4 Data Consistency Across Systems


Ensure alignment of identifiers across policy and claim systems


Validate relationships between policy, claim, and payment data



3.5 Analytical Enablement


Provide structured raw data for downstream KPI computation


Enable Silver and Gold layer transformations
